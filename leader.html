Our Team

Passionate healthcare experts

Mohsin Alvi

Founder
Mohsin Alvi, MD, MBA has more than 20 years of experience in healthcare. A board certified physician, with experience working in, urban, small and rural hospitals in North America including hospitals like Johns Hopkins Hospital, Harvard Medical School and Tufts Medical Center. He was co-founder of chronic disease management platform, he embedded clinical decision support systems in Electronic Medical Records, computerized Provider order entry, hospital process and performance improvement over years. He also severed, as a faculty member at University of Massachusetts Medical School where he taught evidence based medicine practice skills to medical students, residents, advanced practice providers. He is passionate about using health care technology and informatics in safety, quality and bending the cost curve. He has been a speaker on numerous health care topics, he enjoys traveling, cooking and biking when he is not working.

Sarah Suleman

VP Strategy
Dr. Sarah Suleman MD, MPH, is a strategy advisor to healthcare organizations. She currently serves as an advisor to the CLSI FAST program and other early stage healthcare startups. She practiced medicine for several years before receiving her MPH from Mailman School at Columbia University. Her works includes developing data driven strategy, creating research based models of delivery and advancing clinical aspects of product development. She brings a combination of training and experience in clinical medicine, clinical research, and public health to her broad base of consulting and advisory work.
