Why Telehospitalist?

Telemedicine has proven to improve outcomes and bring the benefits of hospital medicine, including improved outcomes and decreased hospital spending.

Acute Care

Improve efficiency and timeliness of care with fewer unnecessary transfers

Community Hospitals

Benefit from an innovative model of care to provide the highest possible quality, in a cost-efficient manner

Access Round the Clock

Serve rural populations with limited access to care and physicians, who respond immediately for overnight and urgent support

Follow Ups

Minimize the number of nighttime visits that daytime physicians must make, and they reduce the need for temporary physicians
