Dr. Syed Mohsin Alvi

Founder and CEO
Dr. Syed Mohsin Alvi has more than 20 years experience of working in healthcare . He received his MBA in Medical Services Management from Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine and completed his medical residency at the University of Connecticut . He has experience of working and learning in more than 20 large, urban, small and rural hospitals in North America including hospitals like Johns Hopkins Hospital, Harvard Medical School and Tufts Medical Center. As a faculty at University of Massachusetts Medical School he had taught evidence based medicine practice skills to medical students, residents, advanced practice providers. He is passionate about using health care technology and informatics in safety, quality and bending the cost curve. He had worked in embedding clinical decision support systems in Electronic Medical Records, designing Computerized Provider Order Entry , hospital process and performance improvement over years and is co founder of health app for chronic disease management platform ( currently in clinical trial). Dr. Alvi lives with his family in Dover, MA and enjoys giving presentations on health care topics, traveling, cooking, biking and playing tennis.

Dr. Sarah Suleman

MD, MPH
Dr. Sarah Suleman MD, MPH, is a strategy advisor to healthcare organizations. She currently serves as an advisor to the CLSI FAST program and other early stage healthcare startups. She practiced medicine for several years before receiving her MPH from Mailman School at Columbia University. Her works includes developing data driven strategy, creating research based models of delivery and advancing clinical aspects of product development. She brings a combination of training and experience in clinical medicine, clinical research, and public health to her broad base of consulting and advisory work.

Ahsan Alvi

MS Technology Management
Mr. Ahsan has held leadership roles in past, where he lead R&D efforts to focus on innovative design and digital transformation across the entire organization. He has been helping organizations to strategically position themselves with technological innovations to stay ahead of the curve. He holds MS Technology Management from Purdue and Engineering from University of Maryland. He loves the outdoors, especially beaches. He likes tennis during the summer and skiing in winter.
