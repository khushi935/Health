EMPOWERING HOSPITALISTS TO GO THE DISTANCE
BRINGING A NETWORK OF HIGHLY QUALIFIED AND DEPENDENDABLE PHYSICIANS TO A HOSPITAL NEAR YOU

Telehealth

Symbian Health connects small and rural communities across the country by empowering its powerful network of physicians to apply their expertise in underserved hospitals where it is needed most.

Using technology to bridge the gap between doctors and patients, Symbian Health helps community and rural hospitals adapt to the evolving healthcare landscape to provide better, more timely care for their patients.

Behind Symbian Health is a team of dedicated physicians and technologists who want to erase healthcare inequities where ever they might appear, starting with the physician shortages in rural and community hospitals across the country.

Benefits

Reduces healthcare costs

Increase efficiency of care delivery, reduce expenses of caring for patients or transporting to another location, and can even keep patients out of the hospital.

Access to care

Beneficial for patients in medically underserved communities and those in rural geographical locations where clinician shortages exist.

Patient satisfaction

Patients love the convenience, flexibility and live care with their providers. More convenient for patients to stay healthy and engaged in their health care.

Using the highest standards of healthcare delivery, our doctors work with on-site nursing staff and community providers to bring inpatient care where it is needed most.

Why Symbian Health?

Symbian Health optimizes service processes with short cycle times by prioritizing velocity and transparency, creating a powerful and expansive system where details are gathered and shared at the right time and right place to generate actionable insights and provide better outcomes.

This allows Symbian partners to anticipate and react to demand in real-time, ensuring they are ready to handle whatever comes their way.

Symbian Health works by complementing existing systems and processes, decreasing the need for infrastructure turnover and increasing the speed of successful implementations.

All this allows Symbian Health to provide a one of a kind operational optimization for hospitalists to best practice medicine at a distance for those who need their services the most.
