TELEHOSPITALIST SERVICES

HELPING MEDICINE GO THE DISTANCE
BRINGING AFFORDABLE, QUALITY CARE TO A HOSPITAL NEAR YOU

We are connecting communities across the country with our roster of qualified physicians who are eager to bring their expertise to hospitals where they are needed most.

Using technology to bridge the gap between doctors and patients, Symbian Health is helping community hospitals provide better, timely care, which have been impacted by physician shortages due to the constantly evolving healthcare landscape.

We are physicians and technologists who want to help underserved Americans get the care they need, when they need it, right in their community.

Virtual Care

Our physicians are determined to close the gap to quality care for patients of critical access, rural, community, and regional hospitals.

Telehospitalist services

Using the highest standards of healthcare delivery, our doctors work with on-site nursing staff and community providers to bring inpatient care where it is needed most.

Benefits

Reduces healthcare costs

Symbian physicians use technology to provide better care from a distance, increasing the efficiency of hospital operations, and improving financial and clinical outcomes.

Access to care

Physician shortages in underserved locations create health risks for patients who may seek care farther away, or even be unable to access the care they need when they need it.

Patient satisfaction

Interacting with their physicians as they would with their family and friends, patients benefit tremendously from doctors who give more attention and communicate better when they are unencumbered from inefficient hospital processes.

The Symbian Way

Empowered by rapid advances in technology and healthcare, Symbian Health has perfected the art and science of telemedicine to bring the benefits of hospital medicine to communities where they are most lacking.
